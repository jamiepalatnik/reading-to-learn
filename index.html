Home

The assignment was: "Create a simple homepage that introduces yourself, your favorite hobby or extracurricular, or anything else of interest to you."

That's pretty broad! So I chose to create a site about reading, which is also broad. My love of reading feels so close to the core of who I am as a person, so I think that discussing how I read will also serve as a personal introduction. I know that reading is not an especially unique love or hobby, but I'll try to share a few details to make it personal.

On this site, I will give some background on my own relationship with reading, go over my own "workflow" for reading and the tools I use, and introduce you to the books blog I started back in 2011 and have more or less kept up ever since.
